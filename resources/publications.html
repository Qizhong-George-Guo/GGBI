How to Use This Section

The publications listed below illustrate contributions across stormwater management, hydrology and hydraulics, estuarine processes, watershed studies, coastal engineering, green–grey–blue infrastructure, and integrated resilience. As more materials are added, this page will become a cross-reference linking publications to specific Framework domains and Application pages throughout the GGBI site.
