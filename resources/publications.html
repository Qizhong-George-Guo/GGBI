Selected Publications

A curated selection of research publications, invited talks, and grey literature relevant to the Green–Grey–Blue–Intelligent (GGBI) Framework and its component domains. This list will be expanded over time to include additional citations and links to full-text documents where possible.

Journal papers Conference papers Invited talks Grey literature Conceptual writing

How to Use This Section

The publications listed below illustrate contributions across stormwater management, hydrology and hydraulics, estuarine processes, watershed studies, coastal engineering, green–grey–blue infrastructure, and integrated resilience. As more materials are added, this page will become a cross-reference linking publications to specific Framework domains and Application pages throughout the GGBI site.
