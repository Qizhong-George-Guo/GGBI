Overview of Available Resources

These pages provide structured access to selected academic works, invited talks, engineering reports, educational materials, and conceptual illustrations relevant to stormwater, hydrology, hydraulics, estuarine processes, watershed studies, coastal engineering, and integrated resilience.

Additional materials will be added over time as part of an evolving archive that connects the GGBI Framework to research, teaching, and applied practice.
