Media & Outreach

Media, Outreach & Public Communication

Media and outreach materials related to the Green–Grey–Blue–Intelligent (GGBI) Framework and to broader topics in stormwater, flooding, estuaries, coasts, and resilience. This includes interviews, invited presentations, webinars, graphics, and public-facing explanations that help connect technical work with wider audiences.

Interviews Invited talks Webinars Graphics Public summaries Educational videos

Purpose of This Section

The goal of this page is to highlight how ideas from the GGBI Framework, and from related research and teaching, have been shared outside traditional journals and technical reports. These materials support knowledge exchange across disciplines and communities, including:

  • Public agencies and practitioners.
  • Students and educators.
  • Community groups and NGOs.
  • Media and general audiences.

Over time, links to selected media pieces, presentations, and graphics can be added here as they are prepared for sharing.

Types of Media & Outreach Materials

Interviews & Articles

News interviews, magazine pieces, and written Q&A articles related to flooding, green infrastructure, coastal resilience, and integrated planning.

Invited Talks & Webinars

Presentations to universities, professional societies, agencies, and other organizations on topics aligned with the GGBI Framework.

Graphics & Conceptual Figures

Diagrams and illustrations used in presentations, classes, and online posts to convey core ideas about Green, Grey, Blue, and Intelligent elements.

Short Videos & Audio Clips

Brief explanations of key concepts, such as green infrastructure, storm surge, estuarine processes, and compound flooding, produced with conventional or AI-assisted tools.

Some media items may be hosted on external platforms (e.g., university pages, professional societies, or video platforms). Where possible, stable links will be provided.
